Screw connectionThe two DOfs of a Cylindric joint can be constrained in a screw-like connection by defining a ratio of translational to rotational motion, called the screw ratio or Output Ratio. This ratio determines how fast the slave rotates as it translates along the joint axis. See the FEDEM Theory Guide, Section 6.4.3 "Screw joint" for more information about the screw ratio. Convert to Prismatic jointClick this button to replace the Cylindric joint with a Prismatic joint connected to the same set of triads. The properties assigned to the Tz DOF will then be transferred to the Prismatic joint, whereas the Rz properties (if any) will be lost.
